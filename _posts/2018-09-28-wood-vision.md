<p style="text-align: center;">We have recently welcomed <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/brand/wood-and-vision">Wood and Vision by PBJ</a> to the <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/">Clickon</a> family who brings with them fifty years of experience in fine craftsmanship. Established in Denmark in 1968, <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/brand/wood-and-vision">PBJ</a> has a tradition of bringing together Certified Cabinet Makers and Furniture Architects to create innovative pieces that are oh so charming.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/">Clickon</a> is honoured to be the sole Australian stockist of this fantastic brand and we were very excited to launch their new range, <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/brand/wood-and-vision">Wood and Vision</a> in our <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/clickon-abbotsford-showroom">flagship showroom</a> recently. It was a tremendous success thanks to our special guest speakers, Interior Design Guru and Influencer, Simone Haag and <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/brand/wood-and-vision">PBJ’s</a> director, Keld Ladefoged Møller Andersen sharing with us their invaluable expertise.</p>

<h3 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Furniture with a Conscience</strong></h3>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/brand/wood-and-vision">PBJ</a> pride themselves on leaving the smallest fingerprint on the environment as possible whilst not sacrificing on quality. They use carefully hand-sorted veneers for a lot of their pieces to reduce the use of solid wood. All timber that is used is tested to ensure it has the correct moisture levels and is sourced from a state approved forestry. They have put in the hard work for you so you can have a beautiful home without the guilt.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">All of the pieces have been exceptionally thought out and ooze functionality whilst still remaining captivating in their Scandinavian aesthetic.</p>

<h3 style="text-align: center;"><strong>City Slicker</strong></h3>
<p style="text-align: center;">The <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/urban-narrow-highboard">Urban Narrow Highboard</a> is an eye catching storage unit that doubles as a desk with its flip down door, making it the perfect piece for inner city living. This piece also makes for a clever hidden bar for those who prefer to entertain.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Like all of their collections, the <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/catalogsearch/result/?q=urban%20board%20wood%20and%20vision">Urban series</a> includes a large range of <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/living-room-furniture/sideboards?product_brand=3161">sideboards</a> in different colours and sizes to cater to any space.</p>
<a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/urban-narrow-highboard"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1889" src="https://blog.clickonfurniture.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/WV-Blog-1-1024x717.jpg" alt="" width="650" height="455" /></a>
<h3 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Fifty Years in the Industry</strong></h3>
<p style="text-align: center;">We have simply fallen in love with the <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/catalogsearch/result/?q=fifty">Fifty collection</a>, nominated for the prestigious German Design Award, <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/brand/wood-and-vision">PBJ</a> designed this series in celebration of their fifty year anniversary.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">The collection is a perfect reflection of mid-century Danish design with the juxtaposition of curved and straight lines. The <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/living-room-furniture/sideboards?product_brand=3161">sideboards</a> and <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/catalogsearch/result/?q=highboard">highboards</a> in the collection offer ample storage and sophistication.</p>

<h3 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Elevating your interior to new lengths</strong></h3>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/brand/wood-and-vision">Wood and Vision</a> have brought with them an innovational selection of <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/catalogsearch/result/?q=extendable%20wood%20and%20vision">Extension Dining Tables</a>. All feature an imaginative and brilliantly designed butterfly extension mechanism that make extending your table a breeze.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Perfect for the Australian lifestyle, an <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/catalogsearch/result/?q=extendable%20wood%20and%20vision">extension dining table</a> is a perfect resolution for smaller spaces without having to sacrifice those cherished get togethers with loved ones.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">The <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/fifty-extendable-dining-table">Fifty Extension Table</a> extends to an impressive 4300mm. Simone Haag has paired the <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/fifty-extendable-dining-table">Fifty Extension Dining</a> Table with the <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/tribe-chair">Tribe Dining Chair</a> in black oak for an on trend yet timeless dining experience.<a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/brand/wood-and-vision"> Wood and Vision</a> have an extensive collection of Scandinavian <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/dining/dining-chairs?product_brand=3161">dining chairs</a> in an array of contemporary styles and colours.</p>

<h3 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Less is More</strong></h3>
<p style="text-align: center;">When it comes to the Danish way of living less is always more. The <a href="https://www.clickonfurniture.com.au/less-wall-unit-1">Less Wall Unit</a> features multiple shelving, cabinet and wall rail options so it is completely customisable to suit your needs.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Fit it around your entertainment system in your living room or make it a focal point on that wall you have been pondering for months.</p>

<h3 style="text-align: center;">Scandinavian vs Australian lifestyle</h3>
<p style="text-align: center;">All of their pieces pay homage to Scandinavian living and have small silhouettes elevated off the ground to allow a flow of natural light to fill your home which is especially important in the European Winter. It is no wonder Australia has taken to this style as it is perfect for our open plan living.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">There is a vast range of timber finishes, laminates and linoleum so you can create a custom piece that reflects your personality and style.</p>
