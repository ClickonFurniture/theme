<h2 style="text-align: center;">M I U R A    C O N C E P T</h2>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-565" src="http://blog.clickonfurniture.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/pc3.jpg" alt="Miura Concept" width="564" height="565" data-wp-pid="565" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-566" src="http://blog.clickonfurniture.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/pc4.jpg" alt="" width="564" height="56" data-wp-pid="566" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: 14px;">Prudence lives by the beach and roams her local rugged coastline everyday with her adorable little Teddy, and when it comes to art, is mostly inspired by her surrounds, and a deep love of all things color.<!--more--> But occasionally, her champagne taste for all things beautiful bubbles to the top, and this particular piece was inspired by her fave vintage car, the Lamborghini Miura 1967 which happened to fly past her on one of her strolls.</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: 14px;">She loved its beautiful line, shape and stately feel. This incident coincided with her day dreaming about her next project for favorite client, Clickon, and so the Miura concept was born.</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: 14px;">She went straight home to the studio and commenced work on this special piece, spending weeks with her layering and colors to get the body of the work just right.</p>
